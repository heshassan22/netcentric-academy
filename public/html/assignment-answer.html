Best Fluffy Pancakes

Ready in 20 minutes · makes 8 pancakes · uses tbsp and tsp measures. A weekend staple — fluffy every time.

Ingredients

  • 200 g plain flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 300 ml milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Method

  1. Whisk the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
  2. Make a well, then beat in the milk, egg and melted butter until just combined — a few lumps are fine.
  3. Cook ladlefuls on a medium pan until bubbles form on top, then flip and cook until golden.
  4. Keep warm while you cook the rest; serve with your favourite topping.
